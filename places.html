Explore the Beauty of Kashmir

The mesmerizing picnic spots of Kashmir

Pahalgam Gulmarg Doodhpathri Sonamarg Dal Lake

Pahalgam

Known as the Valley of Shepherds, Pahalgam is a picturesque destination offering lush green meadows and serene rivers.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, the Meadow of Flowers, is famous for its skiing resorts and the world's highest golf course.

Doodhpathri

Doodhpathri, or the Valley of Milk, boasts rolling meadows and a tranquil environment perfect for relaxation.

Sonamarg

Sonamarg, the Meadow of Gold, offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains and is a gateway to many trekking routes.

Dal Lake

Dal Lake, the Jewel of Srinagar, is known for its shikaras and houseboats, making it a must-visit for every traveler.
